Baily

This is Bailey, a Border Collie/Golden Retriever cross and our main trainer of horses. He goes everywhere we go on the trails, jumps off in the creek, the lake, the ditch, etc., swims, cools off and pops up right in front of the horses, shakes and runs ahead of us, around us, behind us. He has even been known to stand off to the side and count the riders before he runs back to the front of the line again.

×

Rockie

Rockie, the eldest of the pack, is a natural-born leader. His steady temperament and sharp instincts make him the perfect partner for the horses in training. He’s always the first to respond when a young colt gets jittery, keeping the atmosphere calm and focused. Rockie’s deep bark is enough to redirect a horse’s attention, reminding them that they’re in safe, experienced hands – or rather, paws.

×

Frisco

Frisco, with his sleek frame and boundless energy, is the athlete of the group. He weaves effortlessly between the horses, guiding them through their paces and helping them build confidence. His quick turns and sharp eye ensure no horse feels lost or overwhelmed, making him an essential part of the training team.

×

Sammie

Sammie is the heart of the pack, known for his calm demeanor and gentle approach. He has a special way of comforting nervous horses, trotting beside them as they take their first steps in the ring. Sammie’s patient nature helps even the most skittish foals find their footing, proving that a soft nudge can sometimes be as powerful as a strong lead.

×

Copper

Copper takes his role as protector seriously. He watches over the horses with a keen, watchful eye, always ready to step in if a situation starts to escalate. His deep, reassuring bark can diffuse tension in an instant, creating a safe space for the horses to learn and grow.

×

Luna

Luna is the quiet observer, her sharp eyes catching every flicker of movement around the training grounds. She has a natural talent for reading the moods of both horses and humans, sensing when a horse needs a little extra space or a gentle nudge forward. Luna’s calm presence is a comforting force, especially in the early morning mist or the quiet, starlit hours when the farm feels alive with whispered breaths and soft hoofbeats.

×

Daisy

Daisy is the spark of energy that keeps the entire pack on their toes. She thrives in the open pastures, darting around the horses with a joyful energy that’s impossible to resist. Her playful barks and wagging tail remind the horses that every training session has room for a bit of fun, adding a touch of lightheartedness to even the toughest days.

These amazing dogs help us train the horses, and at night, when we head out riding through the forest, we put glowing collars on them. If we ever lose the trail, they always find the way back for us. It’s such a blast—so much fun!

OUR STORY

We raise, buy, polish, and resell horses, and also take consignments. We offer quality weanlings, yearlings, brood mares, & trail horses. Starting in 1983 and going full-time in 1992, we have sold horses all through the nation. We strive to have the best treatment for our animals.
